Writing is about expressing what you truly feel

2022-05-29 2 min read # writing

I have seen many people who are worried about nothing to write, in my views, the first point of writing is to express your true feelings, don't care about how well you write.

I believe that an article filled with true feelings and correct grammar will be popular. People like to read an article written by a flesh-and-blood person, not one cobbled together for traffic and profit.

There is a word: People are willing to follow a person, not a company. They have different aims about writing, the former is about expressing his or her true feelings and thoughts, and the latter usually aims to attain more traffic and profit.

Since I started writing on my blog, I have been encouraging those around me and those I have met online to be bold in expressing their views and ideas. Because I think that we need to think independently to anti those unfair things.

Believe in yourself, we can write invaluable articles.
